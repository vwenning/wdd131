Victoria Wenning

WDD 131-A2

Overview

Purpose

This website will provide a comprehensive directory of farmers markets in Temecula and the surrounding areas. Users will be able to find market locations

Audience

The primary audience includes local residents, farmers, small business owners, and visitors looking for fresh, locally sourced produce and goods.

Dynamic elements

Ideas include: Interactive filtering options to sort markets by day, location, or vendor type. A map displaying market locations using an embedded API. Event highlights that update dynamically based on upcoming schedules. A weather widget to show market-day conditions.
