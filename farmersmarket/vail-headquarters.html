About This Market

The Vail Certified Farmers Market is located at the historic Vail Headquarters in Temecula. This market operates twice weekly, offering local residents and visitors consistent access to fresh, locally grown produce and artisanal products.

As a certified farmers market, all vendors are verified by the Riverside County Agricultural Commissioner and the California Department of Food and Agriculture, ensuring that you're getting authentic, locally-grown products directly from the producers.

Visitor Tips

Best Time to Visit

Early morning offers the best selection, while midday shopping may have shorter lines.

Parking

Free parking is available in the Vail Headquarters lot and surrounding areas.

Payment Methods

Most vendors accept cash. Some vendors also accept credit cards and mobile payments.

Bring Your Own

Don't forget to bring reusable bags to carry your purchases.

Historic Vail Headquarters

The Vail Certified Farmers Market is located at the historic Vail Headquarters, a restored late 1800s cattle ranch that now serves as a vibrant community gathering place. While visiting the farmers market, take time to explore this unique historic site featuring preserved buildings, shops, and restaurants.
